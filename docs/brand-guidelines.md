## 1. Brand Identity

**Booking Broom** is the central operations hub for multi-location cleaning businesses. The brand conveys trust, efficiency, and real-time clarity for managers overseeing bookings across multiple websites.

**Tagline:** Clean bookings, clear control.

## 3. Typography

- **Headings & Body:** Inter (system-ui fallback)
- **Monospace:** JetBrains Mono (IDs, timestamps)

---

## 4. Voice & Tone

- **Professional** — manager-facing, no fluff
- **Clear** — status and actions are obvious at a glance
- **Trustworthy** — reliable for real-time operations

---

## 5. Logo Usage

- App icon: broom + calendar motif on trust blue (#0284C7)
- Minimum clear space: 8px around logo mark
- Do not stretch or recolor the mark arbitrarily
